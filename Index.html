Game Instructions

  • In your turn - roll the dice (at least one) and accumulate the result in "current".
  • you can roll again or click "HOLD" to add the points from "current" and end the turn.
  • Note! if you get 6-6 you will lose all points from "Current" and the turn will go to your opponent.
  • If you managed to reach the target score - you win! if you passed it - you loose!
